Cargo terminal

Home Cargo terminal

There is a cargo terminal at the PII LLC "POINT VISION" company, which was founded on July 13, 2013. The cargo terminal provides such services as Customs warehouses; Closed warehouses; Open storage warehouses; Container yard; Annual capacity of 1,000,000 tons of cargo. Total area of the territory: 350,000 m². Area of customs warehouses: 4,500 m². Open storage area : 70,000 m². Container area : 40,000 m² designed for simultaneous storage of 1,500 containers. Closed storage areas: 3,600 m² equipped with vertical racks and stackers.

A customs warehouse is a certain legal procedure in which goods imported into a country are temporarily stored. The cargo is under the control of the customs authority in a closed, specially equipped room or in an open area. Area of customs warehouses: 4,500 m². A closed warehouse is created by persons who have the status of legal entities that are included in the Register of Vehicle Owners. Closed storage areas: 3,600 m² equipped with vertical racks and stackers.

Container platforms are an important component of container terminals, along with loading and unloading areas of containers from rail and road transport, loading and unloading cargo from containers, covered warehouses. Container area : 40,000 m² designed for simultaneous storage of 1,500 containers.
