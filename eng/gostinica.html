Home Hotel

The Poykent Hotel was opened in 2014 by Point Vision LLC. During the existence of the hotel, its services were used by specialists who participated in the construction of the Kandym gas processing complex from Switzerland, Sweden, India, Ireland, Ukraine, Russia, Belarus, Belgium, China, Korea, France and many other countries. All of them were satisfied with the living conditions and service. Our regular customers are the company "ENTER Engineering Pte Ltd", "ERIELL Group", LLC "Lukoil Uzbekistan Operating Company" and others. The hotel provides stable cold and hot water supply, WI-FI and TV of Russian channels, daily room cleaning and towel change.
