Our Products

Foundation blocks

Today, quite a variety of materials are used to create the foundation of a house. The bases are made of reinforced concrete, metal piles, bricks, aerated concrete and FBS blocks. This material allows you to carry out work on your own with minimal physical costs and even in the absence of construction skills.The foundation of the FBS blocks is characterized by great strength and resistance to high negative and positive temperatures.

Reinforced concrete supports

As we know, power transmission lines are divided into cable and air. The construction of overhead power transmission lines is not complete without the use of various overhead poles. Reinforced concrete poles of power lines are designed and operated in areas with an estimated air temperature of up to -55 ° C. The main element of such supports are centrifuged or vibrated reinforced concrete pillars.

Tray

Fringe catchment trays are used to ensure timely removal of surface effluents of the roadway. They are installed at the junction of the edge of the roadway and the curb, along the roadway. Side trays are made of heavy concrete and are the most important elements of road construction.

ROAD CURB

A curb stone, or as it is also popularly called a curb, is a reinforced concrete product that serves to separate the roadway from the sidewalk or other pedestrian zone. Replacement of road curbs should be carried out every 5 years. The reason for this is the short service life. The fact is that over time, the edges of the road curb are erased, which means that the main protective function of the product is lost. During this period, the strength of the device also decreases.

Reinforced concrete prestressed road slabs

RCP road slabs have one undeniable advantage: during the construction of temporary roads and highways, PD plates can be reused many times.In addition to highways, PD road slabs can be used in the construction of runways and temporary roads.

Сoncrete ring

Well rings (well rings) are reinforced concrete rings, which are wall material, which is the main one for the construction of wells.Wall rings are used for the installation of round wells, underground pipelines of sewer, water and gas pipeline networks. When installing wall rings to create a well, bottom plates and ring overlap plates are used and are made of concrete cl.B15. Fittings are used from 6 to 12 mm.
