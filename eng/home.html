FIE LLC "POINT VISION"

PII LLC "POINT VISION" is a multidisciplinary company that was founded in 2013. The company operates in such areas as: Cargo terminal; Production of reinforced concrete products; Hotel business;

At the moment, the company has 135 employees who are professionals in their field. Our enterprises are located in the Karakul district of Bukhara region. The total area is 45 hectares. Throughout the history of development, reliability and professionalism have been inextricably linked with the name of the company.

OUR SERVICES

Cargo terminal

The cargo terminal at the company PII POINT VISION LLC provides you with the services of loading and unloading, customs clearance of goods and storage of all types of goods in customs and own warehouses and warehouses of open and closed type, as well as a container platform.

Reinforced concrete products

Reinforced concrete products are used in construction for the construction of reinforced concrete structures using precast concrete structures made of reinforced concrete at the plant.

Hotel

The "Poykent" Hotel was opened in 2014 with the support of Point Vision LLC and is intended mainly for the accommodation of specialists seconded to the region for work. However, the doors of the hotel are open to everyone who wants to stay in it.
